Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the Basic Plan?

The Basic Plan provides limited access to essential features. It includes basic support, access to standard tools, and up to 5GB of storage.

How can I upgrade to a higher plan?

You can upgrade to a higher plan through your account settings. Select the desired plan, and follow the payment instructions to gain instant access to additional features.

Is there a free trial available for the Premium Plan?

Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for the Premium Plan, allowing you to explore all features before committing. Simply click "Start Free Trial" on the pricing page.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. Upon cancellation, you’ll retain access until the end of your current billing cycle.

Are there any additional charges apart from the monthly fee?

Our pricing is transparent, with no hidden fees. However, add-ons or custom features may have additional costs, which are clearly outlined during purchase.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit and debit cards, UPI, and net banking. Payments can be made securely through our website or app, ensuring convenience for our users.
